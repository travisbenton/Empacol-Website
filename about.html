The Empacol Story

Empacol Inc. is a family owned and operated business. We specialize in providing a complete range of Packaging Products and Services to meet the special needs of the South Florida Industry. With more than 15 years of experience in the packing and crating business, we have earned an out standing reputation for quality and service.

We are conveniently located two miles from the Port of Miami and the Miami International Airport in a new state-of-the-art facility.

Our vision as we move into the new century is to improve our Crating Services and to expand our Product Line to provide our customers with total satisfaction every time. Our pledge is to improve every day and to help you in all your Packaging and Crating needs.

Whatever the logistics, Empacol is the solution to all your packing needs.
